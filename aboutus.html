About Us

SQL Fundamentals

  • What is SQL Fundamentals?

    SQL Fundamentals is created by a student at RMIT University with a view to help people from all backgrounds to learn the basics of SQL in a fun and comfortable way. Nowadays, SQL is the essence in almost all technical and computational activities, from small or medium companies to big enterprises or large-scale corporations, not to mention the relentlessly increasing amount of data in the current era of digitalisation.

    Therefore, knowing the fundamentals of SQL should equip you with a basic grasp of how the digitalised world is being operated as well as an extremely important skill to get you prepared for standing out in the job market of technical positions. No matter who you are whether you are someone looking for a basic SQL cheat sheet or a starting learner, SQL Fundamentals is here all for you!!

    SQL Fundamentals - We are here to help you conquer any data complexity with SQL

  • What could you expect from SQL Fundamentals?

    With the content in SQL Fundamentals, you can:

    1. Acquire SQL knowledge with quality lessons
    2. Revise your existing SQL knowledge and skills
    3. Find any of your prefered topic with our clearly formmated lessons
    4. A professional way of organizing materials, an engaging implementation of visuals, graphics or dynamic contents
    5. Be familiar with the most prevalent and frequently used SQL statements in the real world
    6. Learn practical ways to apply the SQL lessons in real dataset with detailed examples
    7. Learn with convenience with anywhere-anytime access in your own comfort, regardless of your devices, whether you are using desktops, tablets, or mobile phones
